About Us

Our Story

Education is the process of trial and error, learning by doing, and picking yourself back up after finding out what you thought you knew was wrong.

That’s the story of Horizan.

It began as a project by actual high schoolers that lived through the long and arduous college application process. Horizan has survived both praise and criticism from judges, investors, and other high school students throughout the years. From napkin sketches to whiteboard wireframes to prototypes, from coast to coast conferences, and from the hands of students to the eyes of teachers, we developed a product that spoke directly to students. Horizan has three goals: to be simple, fast, and free. Our motto is to continue striving for the best even in the face of adversity.

Right now we're at the sunrise, but the best is still over the Horizan.
